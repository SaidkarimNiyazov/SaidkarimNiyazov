<!-- ===== VALUES ===== -->
<h2 align="center">🌟 My Values</h2>
<p align="center">
  My actions are guided by four core values: <b>Leadership</b>, <b>Boldness</b>, <b>Love</b>, and <b>Wisdom</b>.<br>
  <br>
  <b>Leadership</b>: Leadership is not about holding a title or issuing commands. It’s about standing firm in tough times, taking responsibility, and prioritizing the well-being of others. A true leader sacrifices personal comfort to dedicate their life to the greater good.<br>
  <br>
  <b>Boldness</b>: Boldness means saying, “I’ll start.” It’s not just about taking risks but preparing wholeheartedly and taking responsibility for challenges. The greatest changes in my life began with such bold steps. Without boldness, nothing changes.<br>
  <br>
  <b>Love</b>: Love is more than feelings—it’s living with heart and infusing every action with care. I feel incomplete without putting my heart into my work. Love means listening, understanding, and being present for others. My family taught me the essence of love—loyalty, patience, and care through time, attention, and action.<br>
  <br>
  <b>Wisdom</b>: Wisdom isn’t just about reading books—it’s about feeling life, pausing to reflect, and foreseeing consequences. It’s about not rushing, not judging, and listening patiently. My best decisions were born in this state of calm reflection.<br>
  <br>
  These values guide every decision I make. When faced with a choice, I ask myself: Does this align with my values? I invite you to ask yourself the same, as values help you discover your inner strength and purpose.
</p>
